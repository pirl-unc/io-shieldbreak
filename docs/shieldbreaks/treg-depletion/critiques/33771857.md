## Per-dimension findings

<details class="sb-section" open markdown="1">
<summary>Show / hide</summary>


### Design

Combined preclinical (ID8 mouse model) + phase 0/I (DD escalation for functional Treg reduction) + phase II (DD monotherapy in ovarian, failed) + phase II (DD + IFNα2a, 2/2 responders before DD shortage halted). Complex multi-study abstract.

### Sample size

Phase II salvage combination: n=2 completed before DD shortage. This is a SINGLE-DIGIT observation. Phase II monotherapy 'failed' — n not cleanly reported in abstract.

### Effect magnitude calibration

DD alone 'failed phase II' in ovarian cancer — a clean negative signal. Adding IFNα2a to DD 'producing immunologic and clinical benefit in two of two patients' — n=2 is an uninterpretable signal. The abstract frames 2/2 as encouraging but statistical noise is indistinguishable from true effect.

### Missing data

DD shortage halted the trial — not a bias issue per se but limits data.

### Multiplicity

Multiple sub-studies conflated in abstract.

### Generalizability

Ovarian cancer; ID8-murine + human phase I-II.

### Treg gating / definition

Abstract refers to 'functional Treg reduction' (suggesting suppression assay used). This would side-step CD25-gating confound; full text required to verify.

### Conflict of interest & funding

Not accessible from abstract.

### Spin / framing

2/2 patients is presented as a positive signal in the abstract. This is mild spin — 2 patients cannot establish efficacy; the DD monotherapy failure is the more interpretable result.

</details>
