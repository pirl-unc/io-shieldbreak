## Per-dimension findings

<details class="sb-section" open markdown="1">
<summary>Show / hide</summary>


### Design

Phase 1 dose-escalation, open-label, single-arm for each drug. NCT04691375 (PY314) / NCT04669899 (PY159).

### Sample size

Small dose-escalation cohorts.

### Effect magnitude calibration

Limited activity in platinum-resistant ovarian monotherapy — consistent with single-agent TAM-targeting not driving tumor regression in cold/immune-excluded tumors.

### Missing data

Reported.

### Multiplicity

Multiple biomarkers; exploratory.

### Generalizability

Platinum-resistant ovarian — immune-cold, difficult-to-treat.

### Gating / definition

Paired-biopsy mIF with CD68/CD163/CD206/TREM2 or LILRB; scRNA on digested biopsies. Strong gating.

### Counter-productive mechanisms

TREM2 is critical for microglial homeostasis — systemic anti-TREM2 could have neurodegeneration implications on long-term dosing (TREM2 loss-of-function mutations cause Nasu-Hakola disease). TREM2 also supports osteoclast function. Short-term dosing unlikely to manifest these; flag for durability.

### Conflict of interest & funding

Pionyr Immunotherapeutics funded (parent company).

### Spin / framing

Measured.
